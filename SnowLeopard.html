Did you know that snow leopards can adapt to hunt at night if the area they are living in is inhabited by humans. If it is not, they hunt at dawn and dusk. They also have really powerful legs that let them jump up to 6 times the length of their body. Although snow leopards are thankfully not in the 'endangered' catagory, and they have moved to the 'velnerable' catagory, they number of snow leopards in the wild are decreasing rapidly due to habitat loss and deforestation.
